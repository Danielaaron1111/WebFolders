Why the Capybara is the Best Boy of the Rodent World

The capybara is basically a dog-sized guinea pig. What’s not to love?

We love these little guys – and you should, too!

As it turns out, the largest rodent in the world also has the most chill...

They spend most of their lives chilling in the grasslands...

Capybara Fast Facts

  • Native to South America
  • An absolute unit
  • Eats a lot of grass*
  • Giving head pats feels like petting a coconut
  • About as clever as a bag of hammers
  • Japanese zoos let them soak in onsen hot springs

* and also their own poop…
